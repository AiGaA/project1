Our story

All it takes - just one carpenter, who fell in love with woodwork and having just an idea and a dream that now has evolved into production. Every little wood piece is made by hand and carved with great care. Passion, patience and love for this work is all it takes to make these saunas to be beautiful, sturdy and unique. We enjoy every minute spent working on these saunas, as the end result just could not be better.
